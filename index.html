Walk. Run. Paddle. Ride. Swim. However you like to move, join the ‘7 Sights Challenge’ to help save Australians from glaucoma blindness. By taking part you’ll not only raise funds for critical patient education and support services that prevent vision loss but you’ll help fund the ground-breaking research that improves treatment outcomes and saves sight.

How it works

1. Register

Sign up as an individual or team, set your fundraising goal and let others know you are on a mission to save sight. Tell your friends to join too!

2. Fundraise

Share your fundraising goal and motivation for getting involved in this fun challenge. Ask everyone you know to support your incredible efforts.

3. Find, Snap and Share

Challenge yourself to discover and snap this year's seven sights. Share your progress and pics and ask your friends to help you smash your fundraising goal!

The Challenge

The 7 Sights Challenge is all about appreciating our precious peepers. Look at what you can see! How amazing is that? Imagine not seeing that ever again, because glaucoma stole your sight.

We are calling on you to join the mission to take glaucoma out of the picture. Because every snap taken and every sight shared reminds us how precious our eyesight really is.

Glaucoma affects 1 in 50 Australians and is the leading cause of preventable yet irreversible vision loss and blindness. Your fundraising efforts will help end glaucoma blindness through early detection, better treatment outcomes and support services that improve quality of life.
